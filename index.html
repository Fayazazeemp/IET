About Us

The Ideal Educational Trust, located near the Government Engineering College in Palakkad, was established in 2003 by students of the college and well-wishers from the Muslim community in Palakkad district. The founders of the Ideal Educational Trust aimed to build a mosque and a hostel attached to it, fostering and sustaining religious devotion among the students of the college. Additionally, the trust was established to work towards the educational, social, cultural, and economic advancement of Muslims in general. Since its inception, the trust has provided invaluable support and resources to students, helping them to thrive academically and spiritually. Over the years, the organization has built a commendable reputation within the community for its dedication and contributions to the advancement of Muslims in Palakkad.

Our Facilities

Mosque

Our mosque offers a serene environment for prayers and spiritual activities. Designed to accommodate a large number of worshippers, it serves as a peaceful sanctuary for our community. The mosque is equipped with modern facilities, including air conditioning, a sound system for clear audio during prayers and sermons, and ample space for men and women to pray comfortably. We also host regular religious classes, seminars, and community events to foster spiritual growth and community bonding.

Hostel

Our hostel provides comfortable and secure accommodation for students. Each room is well-furnished with beds, study tables, and storage facilities. The hostel is equipped with modern amenities such as high-speed internet, laundry services, and recreational areas. We prioritize the safety and well-being of our residents by implementing strict security measures, including CCTV surveillance and 24/7 security personnel. Additionally, our hostel offers a variety of meal plans, ensuring nutritious and balanced diets for our students. Regular cleaning services and maintenance ensure a hygienic living environment, making it an ideal home away from home for our students.
